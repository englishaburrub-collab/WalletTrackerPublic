Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Wallet Tracker app ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it to us, for example by contacting support.

2. Financial Data

Financial data you enter into the app, such as transactions, balances, and categories, is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. Usage Data

We may collect limited anonymous technical information to improve app functionality, such as crash reports or app performance metrics. This information does not directly identify you.

4. Permissions

The app may request permissions that are necessary for app features, such as storage access for backups or exports. These permissions are used only for app functionality.

How We Use Information

Any information processed is used solely to:

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on your device. We do not store your financial data on our own servers. We take reasonable measures to protect information handled by the app.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as Google Play Services or crash reporting tools. Those services may process limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law.

Your Choices

Because app data is primarily stored locally on your device, you can delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app, subject to any backups you created.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
