# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 20, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Wallet Tracker app (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles your information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

We **do not collect any personally identifiable information** such as:

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number

unless explicitly provided by you (e.g., through support contact).

---

### 2. Financial Data

All financial data you enter into the app (such as transactions, balances, and categories):

* Is stored **locally on your device**
* Is **not transmitted to our servers**
* Is fully under your control

---

### 3. Usage Data

We may collect limited anonymous data to improve the app, such as:

* App performance metrics
* Crash reports

This data **does not identify you personally**.

---

### 4. Permissions

The app may request certain permissions (if applicable), such as:

* Storage access (for backups or exports)

These permissions are used **only for app functionality** and not for tracking.

---

## How We Use Information

We use collected data solely to:

* Provide and maintain app functionality
* Improve user experience
* Fix bugs and crashes

---

## Data Storage & Security

* Your data is stored **securely on your device**
* We do not store your financial data externally
* We take reasonable measures to protect your information

---

## Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services (such as analytics or crash reporting tools). These services may collect anonymized data according to their own privacy policies.

Examples may include:

* Google Play Services
* Firebase Analytics (if used)
* Crash reporting tools

---

## Data Sharing

We **do not sell, trade, or share your data** with third parties.

---

## Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally:

* You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
* You have full control over your information

---

## Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date.
